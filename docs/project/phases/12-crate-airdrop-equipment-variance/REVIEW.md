# Phase 12 Plan Review

## Review Summary
- **Overall assessment:** APPROVE-WITH-BINDING-CONDITIONS.
- **What is solid:** The North Star fit is strong: crate vocabulary, deterministic equipment variance, public airdrop pressure, telefrag as a discovered kill-feed mechanic, and honest environmental attribution all align with mental-model pillars 1/5/6/8 (`docs/project/spec/mental-model.md:84`, `:88-91`) and current phase-12 intent (`docs/project/spec/mental-model.md:141-153`). The chosen `environmentalDeaths` channel is the right architecture because `runStats` currently derives top-level kills directly from `resolution.deaths.length` (`convex/engine/runStats.ts:191-193`).
- **What is risky or unclear:** The plan under-inventories the rename blast radius, especially `matches.ts`, validator/tool-schema surfaces, and replay grid/hover/reconstruction files. The airdrop lifecycle wording is off by one turn for this engine's input/resolve boundary. The separate `airdrops[]` model also needs a precise landed-crate loot/update path before WP-C/D implementation.

## Spec / Guide Deviations
- The plan's lifecycle table deviates from the engine's turn contract: inputs are built from start-of-turn state before `resolveTurn`, so "landed on `turn >= landsAtTurn`" is not a safe projection rule for the landing turn.
- The plan's blast-radius table does not satisfy the North Star's "engine, vision, system prompt/tool surface, personas, id-normalisation, replay UI, diagnostics, reports, tests, specs" scope until `matches.ts`, `validation.ts`, `decisionTool.ts`, and replay grid/hover/reconstruct files are added.
- The plan's mental-model citations are stale. The authoritative current intent is mental-model §11, not the cited §16-§20.
- Historical phase report freezing is acceptable under POC/closed-phase precedent, but the acceptance wording must avoid implying a full-repo grep-clean gate.
